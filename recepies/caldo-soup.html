Caldo de Pollo

Caldo de pollo or chicken Soup besides being a very healthy food, is contains many nutrients
that the body needs to stay healthy. It is so delicous and easy to make. It has many benefits and tastes so good.

You can also make some rice and combine it with the soup for a more
tastefull experience and of course, do not forget the chile sauce which
gives it an awsome hot flavor.

Ingredients

  • Whole Chicken
  • Corn
  • Carrots
  • Calabaza
  • Garlic
  • Chayote
  • Cilantro
  • yelow squash
  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • tomatoes

Cookin Directions

  1. Put water in a large bowl
  2. Heat and then put salt, garlic and onion
  3. Let it boil for 20 min in medium heat
  4. Add the all other ingredients except potatoes to boiling water
  5. After 30 minutes add the potatoes
  6. Leave it for 10 minutes in low heat
  7. Stir and taste to liking.
  8. At the end, add tomatoe sause or paste
